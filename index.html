Intro

Hi, my name is Huynh Dong, and I’m a Data Analyst. I hold a Bachelor’s and PhD degree in Chemical Engineering (Oil & Gas, Paris Nord University, Paris 6 University and IFP School, France). Over last 10 years, I have always enjoyed data, figures and correlation. So that’s how I look at data sets: to me it is talking numbers I want to speak their language. Finding patterns nobody else sees is the challenge to me, that’s my passion. Before I started as a Data Analyst, I had worked for more than teen years as a data researcher, chemical researcher, process engineer in technology consulting and chemical process operation & maintenance. I have worked for several oil & gas projects and companies which gives me the ability to translate complex questions into understandable insights. Do you want to work together? Please reach out to me by e-mail. Check out my work.

In this project, different factors that contribute to Final Sale Price of Real Estate for Mortgage Backed Securities Investment Allocation have been analyzed. Key factors have been recommended in order to understand how it should allocate dollars earmarked for investment into mortgage-backed securities. The raw data that was provided originally came from Kaggle. The project has been implemented using Tableau and summarized in PowerPoint deck. The process covering: (1) Orienting myself to the data conducting exploratory data analysis. (2) valuating the distribution of variables to advance the business objective. (3) Assumption and testing hypothesis using inferential statistics to reject or support my hypothesis and (4) Build a PowerPoint presentation deck to communicate those insights. (Used Tableau to build Visualizations).
